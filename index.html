BIO!

St. Francis Xavier High School is located at , Duler Mapusa in North Goa district of Goa, India. It is affiliated with CBSE - Central Board of Secondary Education. St. Francis Xavier High School is a high secondary school,primary school,private school,. It believes in overall development of the students by balancing academics, extra curricular activities, sports in the state of art infrastructure within the campus. The school offers playgrounds for cricket, basketball, volleyball, table tennis, badminton and other sports. The admission process for this School starts every year in the month of May and School starts in July.
